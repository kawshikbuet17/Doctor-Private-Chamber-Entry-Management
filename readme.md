## Connection

9 - Reset\
10 - VCC\
11 - GND\
14 - RXD = Bluetooth module\
15 - TXD = Bluetooth module\
16 ~ 21 = LCD4bit \
22 - SCL\
23 - SDA\
30 - AVCC = VCC\
31 - GND\
32 - AREF = 1 micro F with GND\
39 - ADC1 = palse sensor analog input\
40 - ADC0 = temp sensor analog input\
